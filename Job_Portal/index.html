Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How do I apply for a job?

Click on the "View Details" button for any job listing, then follow the application instructions provided by the employer.

Is it free to use this platform?

Yes, our basic job search and application features are completely free for job seekers.

How do I save jobs to favorites?

Click the heart icon on any job card to save it to your favorites. You can view all saved jobs in the Favorites tab.

Can I upload my resume?

Currently, we support direct application through employer links. Resume upload functionality is coming soon!
