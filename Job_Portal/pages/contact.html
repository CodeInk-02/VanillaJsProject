Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Simply click on the "Join" button in the navigation and fill out the registration form with your details.

How do I apply for a job?

Browse through our job listings, click on any job that interests you, and use the "Apply Now" button to submit your application.

Can I save jobs for later?

Yes! You can save jobs to your favorites by clicking the heart icon on any job card. View all your saved jobs in the "Favorites" tab.

How do I update my profile?

Log into your account and go to your profile settings. You can update your information, skills, experience, and portfolio at any time.

Is the platform free to use?

Yes, our basic features are completely free! We also offer premium features for advanced users and employers.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team through the contact form above, email us at support@jobportal.com, or call us during business hours.
